About Us

Manufacturing shaped by detail, trust, and global standards.

Hawthrone Apparel exists for buyers who need more than capacity. We combine careful workmanship, transparent communication, and export knowledge so every production run moves with confidence.

Our Position

A premium manufacturing partner for serious apparel brands.

We support buyers from concept development through final shipment, helping translate design intent into commercially reliable garments. Every order is handled with clear sampling records, production planning, inspection reports, and packing requirements aligned to the buyer's market.

Our team works best with brands that value construction integrity, refined finishing, and accountable timelines.

"We treat each product as a brand asset, not simply a unit on a line."

Production philosophy

Values

The standards behind every partnership.

Craft Focus

Fit, stitching, finishing, hand feel, trim placement, and presentation are reviewed with premium retail expectations in mind.

Transparent Flow

Sampling, approvals, production status, inspections, and shipment milestones are communicated clearly across the order lifecycle.

Export Readiness

Commercial invoices, packing lists, carton markings, labels, and buyer-specific requirements are prepared for international dispatch.

Partnership Flow

How we move from brief to shipment.

  1. 01

    Brief Review

    We review tech packs, references, materials, target cost, MOQ, size range, market, and delivery window.

  2. 02

    Sampling

    Prototype, fit, salesman, or pre-production samples are developed for structured approvals.

  3. 03

    Production

    Materials, trims, cutting, stitching, finishing, and packing are managed against a production calendar.

  4. 04

    Export Dispatch

    Final inspection, carton planning, buyer documents, and shipping coordination prepare the order for export.
