Quality Control

Inspection systems built for premium apparel exports.

Our QC process combines material checks, measurement control, in-line inspections, final audits, and export packing review to protect buyer standards.

01

Incoming Material Check

Fabric quality, shade, shrinkage direction, trims, labels, accessories, and buyer-approved references are checked before bulk release.

02

In-Line Inspection

Stitching, measurements, seam strength, construction details, placement, and workmanship are monitored during production.

03

Final Audit

AQL-based checks review appearance, sizing, labeling, packing, carton marking, and approved sample conformity before dispatch.

Compliance Mindset

Documented control from sample approval to packed carton.

For export buyers, quality depends on both craftsmanship and evidence. Our records help track approvals, tolerances, defects, corrective actions, packing details, and shipment readiness.

  • Measurement tolerance control
  • Color and shade lot review
  • Print, embroidery, and trim placement checks
  • Packing, labeling, and carton audit
  • Buyer-specific inspection standards
