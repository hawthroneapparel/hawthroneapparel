Manufacturing

Structured production for premium garments and export schedules.

Our manufacturing workflow is designed to protect product intent, reduce surprises, and keep buyers informed from first sample through final packed carton.

01

Sampling & Development

Tech pack review, pattern coordination, fabric trials, fit samples, size-set samples, and pre-production approvals.

02

Material & Trim Sourcing

Fabric, trims, labels, buttons, zippers, drawcords, embroidery, printing, washing, and finishing aligned to buyer standards.

03

Cutting & Sewing

Planned cutting, line loading, stitching, measurement control, in-line checks, and production status reporting.

04

Finishing & Packing

Thread trimming, pressing, folding, tagging, polybagging, carton packing, barcode placement, and final shipment handover.
